B.3.3 Graphical Terminals

You can also start a terminal in a graphic window if you click on the little tv looking icon down on the panel bar. (see fig B.1) Graphical terminals have one advantage over the function key terminals. You can scroll back or up into the history of the commnds that you have issued and read all of the response from the command.
