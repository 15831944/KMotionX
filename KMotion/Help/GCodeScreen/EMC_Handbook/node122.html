B.2 Point and click with the KDE desktop environment.

Point-and-click is a bit different in most of the Linux graphic environments from what you may be used to with your old operating system. You only need to click once with the left mouse button to start up whatever is under the mouse pointer. If you double click, most of the time you will get two of 'em. Trust me, this takes some getting used to.
